Common questions

  • How do I play? Tap How to Play on the main menu for the full rules — roll five dice, hold the ones you like, and fill all 13 scorecard categories.
  • How do I play with friends? Tap Create Multiplayer Game and share the room code or link. Friends join instantly from their phone.
  • What is Yam World? A solo campaign across four themed worlds. Beat stages to earn credits and spend them on power-ups in the shop.
  • My progress or credits look wrong. Make sure you're signed in (Google or Apple) — progress is saved to your account and follows you across devices.
  • How do I delete my account? Use the delete account page or the in-app option in your profile. This permanently removes your data.
