Delete Your Yamio Account

Last Updated: May 8, 2026

You can delete your Yamio account and all associated data right here on this page. No app install or email is required.

Option 1 — Delete now from this page (recommended)

Click the button below to sign in with the Google account you use for Yamio and delete your data immediately. This permanently removes your account, credits, skins, achievements, and stats.

What this does: Signs you in with Google, removes your record from our database, and permanently deletes your Firebase Authentication account. The page will confirm when deletion is complete.

Option 2 — Delete from inside the app

  1. Open Yamio
  2. Sign in with Google
  3. Tap your profile, then Delete account
  4. Confirm — your data is removed immediately

Option 3 — Email request

If neither option works for you, email us with the subject "Delete My Account":

📧 Email support@yamio.io
Processing time: Email requests are processed within 30 days of receipt. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete. Options 1 and 2 are immediate.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your Yamio account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your account information (name, email, profile picture from sign-in)
  • Your in-game username
  • Your game scores and statistics
  • Achievements you have unlocked
  • Daily challenge progress and streaks
  • In-game credits and skin purchases
  • Any active multiplayer room data
  • Device-specific local profile data

What Data May Be Retained

For legal, security, or technical reasons, some limited data may be retained for a short period after account deletion:

  • Anonymized analytics — aggregated, non-identifiable data may be retained for product improvement (cannot be linked back to you)
  • Multiplayer game records — game session data is automatically deleted within 24 hours of the game ending, regardless of account status
  • Backup systems — data may persist in encrypted backups for up to 90 days before being permanently deleted

Partial Data Deletion

If you only want to delete specific data (rather than your entire account), please email support@yamio.io and specify exactly what data you would like removed. Examples include:

  • Reset game progress and statistics
  • Clear achievement history
  • Remove username from leaderboards

Partial deletion requests are also processed within 30 days.
